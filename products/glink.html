Market Positioning

Beyond Wi-Fi. Beyond LoRa.

G-Link occupies the gap between consumer Wi-Fi (high throughput, short range) and LPWAN (long range, low throughput) — delivering both, with IP-native networking and standards-based interoperability.

  • Sub-1 GHz spectrum.Better wall penetration, less congestion.
  • IP-native.Standards-based, no vendor gateway lock-in.
  • Massive scale.Thousands of endpoints per access point.
